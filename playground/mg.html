Pratt Institute Graphic Design Intensive

Redesigning the packaging for tomato paste.

Overview

A little about the project

During the Pratt Institute Graphic Design Intensive, to choose which product to redesign, we just walked around a nearby grocery store and picked something up. This made for an interesting process as I had no prior knowledge of the brand or its mission, and only a day to finish it.

Initial Research

Dissecting the brand based on its current packaging

As you can see in the image at the bottom, the package had a rather unappealing image of tomato paste. Upon a closer look, I found that the product was BPA free, Non-GMO, organic, vegan, and gluten free, and checking their website also showed their committment to these. The package, however, did very little to highlight all of this, especially since most of this information was hidden at the back of the can, and the design itself didn't help either (courtesy of the paste). The colours also did not make it stand out on the shelf against its competitors.
